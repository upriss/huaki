Mandolin Sheet Music

The composers listed here did not typically compose mandoline music. But their music was popular 100 years ago and therefore had been arranged for mandolin orchestra during that time.

Composers of the Salon Music genre or who died after 1900

  • Adolphe Adam (1803-1856): Nürnberger Puppe
  • Aletter (1867-1934): Rococo Gavotte
  • Badarzewska (1823-1861): Maiden's Prayer
  • Carl Burow (possibly 1809-1874 but more likely military composer Emil Burow 1855-1943): Polnisches Lied
  • Czibulka (1842-1894): Stephanie Gavotte
  • Leo Delibes (1836-1891): Pizzicati aus dem Ballett "Sylvia"
  • L. C. Desormes (1840-1898): Mandolinen-Polka
  • De Stefano: Flower from Italy
  • Eberle (1853-1930): Paraphrase über "Ein Vöglein sang im Lindenbaum" and another version
  • Eilenberg (1848-1927): Petersburger Schlittenfahrt
  • Ertl (1857-1911): Hoch und Deutschmeister Marsch
  • Fetras (1854-1931): Frohsinn auf den Bergen, Mondnacht auf der Alster
  • Hermann Finck (1872-1939): Schattenspiele
  • Förster (1860-1894): Mandolinen-Serenade
  • Friedemann (1862-1952): Paraphrase über "Aus der Jugendzeit"
  • Emil Fritzsch (Hannoverian composer, musician and music teacher, born before 1860, probably a relative of the editor of the Musikalisches Wochenblatt, E. W. Fritzsch): Serenade
  • Fucik (1872-1916): Die lustigen Dorfschmiede
  • Godard (1849-1895): Berceuse from the Opera "Jocelyn"
  • Denis Granado (1845-1915, leader of the bandurria ensemble "Estudiantina Figaro" which performed at the 1878 World Exhibition in Paris and started the Golden Age of the mandolin): El Turia Valse
  • Heins (1859-1923): Liebesgeflüster (Gavotte)
  • Ivanovici (1845-1902): Donauwellen Walzer
  • Jessel (1871-1942, because he was born Jewish he was barred from work by the Nazis, tortured and subsequently died.): Der Rose Hochzeitszug
  • Klose (1859-1924): Goldiges Lachen
  • Otto Kockert (1865–1941): Amoretten-Ständchen
  • Komzak (1850-1905): Volksliedchen und Märchen
  • Langer (1843-1877): Großmütterchen
  • Maillart (1817-1871): Das Glöckchen des Eremiten
  • Martinez (1875-1914): Auf hohem Meere
  • Meyer-Helmund (1861-1932): Ballgeflüster
  • Franz Moritz, probably Franz Philipp Moritz (1872-1940, pianist, composer and music teacher. He was barred from work by the Nazis because of his Jewish background. I cannot find any other information about him.): Marionetten
  • Noack (1893-1945): Heinzelmännchens Wachtparade
  • Max Oscheit (1880-1923): Gnomen Tanz
  • Peter (1866-1942): Der Kreuzfidele Kupferschmied
  • Radeck (1828-1903): Fridericus Rex Grenadier March
  • Raff (1822-1882): Kavatine
  • Reh: Gavotte Royale
  • Rosas (1868-1894): Über den Wellen
  • Rubinstein (1829-1894): Melodie
  • Rupprecht (1873-1934): Prisca aus der Operette: "Die Najaden"
  • Satie: 1. Gymnopedie
  • Schäffer (1808-1874): Die Post im Walde
  • Schmeling (1864-1943): Ein Abend in Toledo
  • Schrammel (1850-1893): Wien bleibt Wien
  • Willem Frederik Siep (1866-1926): Oud Hollandsche Boerendansen
  • Sullivan (1842-1900): Barataria Marsch
  • Titl (1809-1882): Serenade
  • Toselli (1883-1926): Serenata
  • Translateur (1875-1944, He founded the "Lyra" music publishing company in Berlin. The Nazis forced him to sell the company in 1933. He was deported to the concentration camp Theresienstadt where he died.): Was Blumen träumen
  • Urbach (1872-1927): Aus Mozarts Reich
  • Von Blon (1861–1945): Frühlings-Einzug March
  • Waldteufel (1837-1915): Herbstweisen
  • Yoshitomo (1869-1935): Japanischer Laternentanz
  • Yradier (1809-1865): La Paloma
  • Zeller (1842-1898): Sei nicht bös! aus "Der Obersteiger", Adam Walzer aus der Operette "Der Vogelhändler"
  • Zikoff (1824-1877): Romaneska

    Classical or more well known composers

  • Bach, Christian: Jubel Ouvertüre
  • Beethoven: Die Himmel rühmen, Menuett in G-dur
  • Bizet (1838-1875): Carmen Fantasie
  • Boccherini: Menuett
  • Brahms: Beruehmte Ungarische Tänze, Hungarian Dance Nr 5, Hungarian Dance Nr 6
  • Chopin: Waltz in A Minor
  • Flotow: Potpourri aus der Oper Martha
  • Händel: Dances from the Fireworks Music
  • Haydn: Andante from the Surprise Symphony, Menuett from the symphony in D major, Menuett, Berühmtes Largo (Fis dur)
  • Offenbach: Barcarolle from "Hoffmanns Erzählungen"
  • Mendelssohn: Chanson de printemps, Hochzeitsmarsch
  • Meyerbeer (1791-1864): March
  • Mozart: Menuett aus der Es-dur Sinfonie, Menuett from Don Juan, An die Zither, Zufriedenheit
  • Rossini: Fantasy over Rossini's La gazza ladra
  • Schubert: Moment Musical, Moment Musical, Balletmusik aus Rosamunde, Serenade
  • Strauss: An der schönen blauen Donau, Annen-Polka, Frühlingsstimmen Walzer, Schatzwalzer
  • Verdi: Fantasy over Verdi's Rigoletto, Fantasy over Verdi's Troubadour
  • Wagner: Walthers Preislied
  • Weber: Ouverture of Weber's Oberon

    Ragtime or South American Composers

  • Joplin: Entertainer
  • Cobb (1886-1942): March of the Walking Dolls
  • Rodríguez (1897-1948): La Cumparsita
